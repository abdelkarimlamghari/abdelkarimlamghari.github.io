# about

About me

I am Abdelkarim Lamghari, an applied mathematician and educator specializing in disease modeling and mathematical applications in real-world problems. I enjoy solving complex problems through mathematical modeling and programming, aiming to bring clarity and practical solutions to academic and public health challenges.

My goal is to leverage mathematics to address critical issues in public health, while also inspiring the next generation of mathematicians through education and collaboration.
